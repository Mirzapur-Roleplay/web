Mirzapur

A NoPixel inspired server with our own twist on things! Our management team are continuously researching and developing plugins to implement into the server to keep the roleplay fresh and enjoyable.
We aim to see our players create roleplay situations that are great to be involved with but also entertaining to watch and bring people together, creating new friendships and online families.
